Why Convert Image Formats?

Different image formats serve different purposes. JPEG (JPG) is great for photographs with small file sizes, PNG supports transparency and is lossless, WebP offers superior compression, and GIF is ideal for simple animations. Converting between these formats can help you achieve the perfect balance between quality and performance for your specific needs.

Image Conversion FAQs

Is there a limit to the image size I can convert?

Our tool can handle most standard image sizes, but very large images (over 20MB) may take longer to process or could be limited by your device's memory.

Do you keep copies of my images?

No. All image processing happens in your browser. We never upload your images to our servers, ensuring complete privacy.

Which format is best for web use?

WebP generally offers the best compression with good quality. For maximum compatibility, JPEG is good for photos and PNG for graphics with transparency.
